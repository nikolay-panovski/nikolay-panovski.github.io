Research: Game Juice
Overview

In this project, I resarched effects associated with evoking "visceral" feelings in players by providing additional audiovisual feedback, commonly known as "game feel/juice" effects. I then picked and applied some of them to fix issues previously brought up by user feedback, and others to further enhance the games and test if people would enjoy them more.
Tests did not reveal statistically significant improvements in the overall enjoyment from the games, however the special effects made interactions in Beans' Adventure clearer and more exciting. For a comprehensive list of implemented (and unimplemented) changes and special effects, see this document.
