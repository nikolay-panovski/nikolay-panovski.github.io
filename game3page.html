Upon Slimes
Overview

This project required us to make a physics-based puzzle platformer. We combined a few concepts and arrived at a game with 2 characters (can be played multiplayer or singleplayer) with unique abilities - a rock/size slime and a light/gravity slime. They must interact with the presented object in a proper way to proceed to the end of the level.

v1.0 (Project hand-in)
Tasks during the project:

  • Vector-handled movement, collisions (simple box/box)

  • Object interactions

  • Menu functionality (main and pause menus)
