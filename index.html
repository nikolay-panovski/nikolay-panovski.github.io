About me

Hello! I'm Nikolay Panovski, studying Creative Media and Game Technologies at Saxion University in the Netherlands. I am a second year "Engineer" student, and as such am mainly working on the programming side of games. So far I have experience in C#, Unity, Java (through Processing), and basic HTML+CSS+JS for websites. Looking forward to learning C++ and Unreal Engine, stay tuned for projects showcasing that!


I have worked on individual and team projects, mainly dealing with creating good collisions and interactions, but also getting experience in giving good visual feedback for whatever the users do.
