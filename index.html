About me

Hello! I'm Nikolay Panovski, studying Creative Media and Game Technologies at Saxion University in the Netherlands. I am a first year "Engineer" student, and as such am mainly working on the programming side of games. So far I have experience in C#, Java (through Processing), HTML+CSS.


I have worked on individual and team projects, mainly dealing with creating good collisions and interactions, but also getting experience in returning feedback to the users. Check out my work below!

Portfolio
Upon Slimes

A physics-based puzzle platformer where two players must collaborate with their unique abilities to reach the end door together!


My main tasks were the physics and the interactions. I also did the functionality of the menus.


Language: C#
Engine: GXPEngine (proprietary)
Duration: 2 weeks
Team size: 7 (2 engineers)

Beans' Adventure: Tower of Trickery

A point-and-click game where the player has to help Beans the Cat solve puzzles and retrieve the pieces of her telescope in time for the full moon.


Notably, I set up an inventory system, the input and its handling within the puzzles, and dialog boxes.


Language: Java#
Engine: Processing
Duration: 2 weeks
Team size: 6 (1 engineer)

Outbreak Outlaws

A 2-player arcade game in space/desert style, with a twist - the players must cooperate to win instead of competing! Featuring both platforming and shooting elements.


My focus during this project were the collisions and interactions, however I also handled the UI/scoring and the basic game loop (start screen/game over).


Language: C#
Engine: GXPEngine (proprietary)
Duration: 2 weeks
Team size: 7 (1 engineer)
